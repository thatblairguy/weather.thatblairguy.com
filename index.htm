Silly Weather Reports

An assortment of local weather reports, but rewritten in styles such as Shakespearean sonnets, entries in the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy, limericks and so forth.

How it works: Weather reports are retrieved from the US National Weather Service, rewritten by a copy of the llama AI running on my PC, and then copied to the web.

Reports are generated on the hour, subject to various service outages.
